AgroPulse is a real-time IoT management platform built for greenhouse operators and farm administrators. It connects to ESP32 devices to collect live sensor data — temperature, humidity, CO₂, soil moisture, light, and pressure — and gives you the tools to act on that data through automated rules, manual actuator control, and an AI agronomic assistant.

## What AgroPulse does

AgroPulse bridges the gap between physical greenhouse hardware and actionable farm intelligence. At its core, it polls your ESP32 microcontrollers every few seconds and surfaces the readings in a clean, role-aware dashboard. Beyond monitoring, it lets you:

- **Automate** actuators (pumps, fans, LEDs, relays) when sensor values cross thresholds you define
- **Alert** the right people by email or WhatsApp (via CallMeBot) when conditions deviate from your crop's ideal range
- **Analyze** historical trends with time-series charts and generate downloadable reports
- **Ask AI** — powered by Groq LLaMA 3.1, GitHub GPT-4o mini, or Google Gemma 4 — for tailored agronomic advice based on live sensor context

## User roles

AgroPulse has two access levels:

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Admin** | Full access to all greenhouses, user management, device configuration, logs, and settings |
| **Operator** | Access only to the greenhouses they have been assigned by an admin |
